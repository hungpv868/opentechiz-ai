Our Process

From Discovery Call to Live System in 4–8 Weeks

The Five-Phase Process

1

Discovery & AI Audit

Week 1

We run a structured 30-minute call focused on your highest-volume workflows. We ask about your current tools, your team's manual tasks, your integration points, and your objectives.

Client does

30-minute call, access to basic workflow documentation

You receive

Written AI audit document — prioritized automation opportunities, realistic scope, and what's buildable. No commitment required to receive this.

2

Scope & Architecture

Week 2

We translate the audit into a technical architecture. Integration points, data flows, error handling approach, and delivery timeline — all defined before a line of code is written.

Client does

Review and approve the architecture document

You receive

Signed scope document + full architecture diagram. This is the contract. No surprises after this point.

3

Build & Integrate

Weeks 3–6

We build to the approved architecture. Connected to your live systems, tested against your real data, reviewed by your team at key milestones.

Client does

Provide system access credentials, attend midpoint check-in

You receive

Working system in a staging environment — connected to your real tools

4

QA & Acceptance

Week 7

We run the system through edge cases, load scenarios, and failure modes. You test against your acceptance criteria.

Client does

Run acceptance tests, flag any issues

You receive

QA report + sign-off checklist. Issues resolved before production.

5

Deploy & Handover

Week 8

We deploy to production, verify all integrations are live, and run a team walkthrough covering operation and maintenance.

Client does

Confirm production access, attend handover session

You receive

Live production system + full documentation + operational runbook

What You Receive at Every Stage

No black box delivery

You see every milestone. You approve the architecture before we build.

Scope locked before build

Complexity identified and priced at architecture stage. No mid-project surprises.

You own everything

Code, documentation, architecture diagrams, runbooks. All delivered at handover.

Post-launch support

We don't disappear at go-live. 30-day support period included on every project.
