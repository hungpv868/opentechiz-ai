The Cost of Workflows Still Needing a Human in the Middle

Slow first-touch on inbound leads

Leads that don't hear back in under 5 minutes convert at a fraction of the rate of those who do. After hours, the gap is worse.

Support backlog from repetitive tier-1 tickets

80% of support volume is the same 20 questions. Your team is qualified to do more than copy-paste answers.

Operational tasks that stall when someone's unavailable

If a process requires a human to notice, decide, and act — it doesn't run while they're asleep, on leave, or overwhelmed.

Manual decisions at scale

100 orders per day is manageable. 1,000 isn't — not if each one requires a human check.

What We Build: Agents for Specific Operational Roles

Customer Support Agent

Reads incoming tickets, classifies by type and urgency, resolves tier-1 autonomously, routes tier-2+ with full context summary to the right human. Integrated into Zendesk, Freshdesk, or Intercom.

Lead Qualification Agent

Activates on new lead entry, enriches the CRM record, scores against your ICP, sends a personalized first-touch in under 60 seconds, and routes qualified leads to the right rep with a brief.

Internal Operations Agent

Routes tasks, answers team questions from your actual data, triggers actions based on defined conditions. Available in Slack or Teams, connected to your real systems.

Data Retrieval Agent

Answers questions from your actual databases, documents, and systems. Returns sourced answers — not hallucinations. Integrated into your team's existing communication channels.

Custom Workflow Agent

Scoped to your specific process. We define the trigger, the decision logic, the actions, and the escalation paths — then build and deploy the agent to your environment.

What Makes Our Agents Work in Real Business Systems

Tool Use & System Integration

Memory & Context

Agents maintain context across a session or across multiple interactions. They know the history.

Multi-Step Reasoning

Multi-Agent Orchestration
